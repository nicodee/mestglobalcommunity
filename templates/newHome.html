We invite professionals having expertise in certain fields (e.g. email marketing, Branding, Infrastructure Scaling, Feature phone expertise etc.) to help specific problems or challenges our companies are facing. Mentors are also encouraged to provide ongoing support in their field.

Commitment:
From a one hour phone call to a longer term commitment. Meet one of our Field Mentors Andrew Gottlieb

Our top companies need extra resources are needed to help them better analyze markets, evaluate competition, research channel partners, prospect investment opportunities and propose strategy recommendations. MBA students and recent business school graduates are invited to work with our companies. MBA consultants are encouraged to meet the entrepreneurs weekly via phone, e-mail and Skype from their location in the U.S. or Europe, and are offered the opportunity to travel to Accra at least once to conduct a workshop and meet the entrepreneurs in person.

Responsibilities
Understand the start-up businesses: current assets, business model and growth options. Provide feedback and insight on competitive analysis, market research, financial projections and business strategy. Work with the companies on fundraising, preparing pitch decks and to help position themselves for attractive valuations in future rounds. Make argued recommendations on the best growth option to the start-ups board and executive team.

The MBA consultant will be working closely with the company CEO (based in Ghana) and MEST incubator staff (based in SF).

Commitment
Average commitment ranges from 2 to 4 hours a week + 1 week on ground in Accra. Meet one of our MBA Consultants Andrei Stephens

Experts in Residence (EiR) program is a new initiative that gives mid-career professionals the opportunity to make a significant impact in Africa by contributing their expertise to our program. As an expert in residence you will augment the expertise of the resident teaching fellows with your 5-10 years of real-world experience in your field.

EiR should have an entrepreneurial spirit, the desire to help people, and a sense of adventure. You will be working within a team with one mission: help MEST students/ companies create globally successful software companies.

Our program is fast, flexible, and innovative so the skills you will learn will give you an advantage in whatever you decide to do next.

Commitment
6-8 weeks in Accra, Ghana. Housing and meals provided. Meet one of our Experts in Residence Susannah Raub
